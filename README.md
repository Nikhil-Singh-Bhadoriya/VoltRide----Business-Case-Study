# 🚀 VoltRide Business Case Study

## 📊 Overview

This project analyzes ride-hailing operations data (2,500+ rides across
Bengaluru, Delhi, Hyderabad, and Mumbai) to uncover revenue leakage,
demand patterns, and optimization opportunities.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 🔍 Key Objectives

-   Identify revenue loss due to ride cancellations\
-   Analyze city-wise and zone-level demand patterns\
-   Forecast ride demand using statistical and machine learning models\
-   Provide actionable business recommendations

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 📈 Key Insights

-   \~29.5% ride cancellation rate leading to ₹337K+ revenue loss\
-   Hyderabad & Bengaluru contribute the highest revenue leakage\
-   Demand varies significantly across zones and time slots\
-   Potential to recover \~₹71K revenue using targeted interventions

------------------------------------------------------------------------
